# US-04: Mark Attendance for Participating Members

**Epic:** Attendance & Input

**As a** team captain,
**I want to** check or uncheck each member's attendance for the session,
**so that** only those who actually played are included in the cost split.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each member row has a checkbox to mark participation.
- [ ] All members are unchecked by default (except the payer, per US-02).
- [ ] Checking a member's box makes the hours and guests fields for that member active/editable.
- [ ] Unchecking a member clears and disables their hours and guests fields.
- [ ] At least one member must be checked before proceeding to Review.
