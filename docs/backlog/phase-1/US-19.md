# US-19: Player Self-Registration for a Match

**Epic:** Match Registration

**As a** player,
**I want to** tap my avatar or name on the match detail page to confirm I am joining,
**so that** the captain knows who is coming without needing to take attendance manually.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The match detail page (`/matches/[id]`) shows the full member roster as avatar tiles.
- [ ] Tapping an avatar registers that member for the match (calls `POST /api/matches/[id]/register`).
- [ ] A registered member's avatar shows a visual confirmation (checkmark, green border, or filled state).
- [ ] Tapping a registered avatar a second time unregisters the member (calls `DELETE /api/matches/[id]/register`).
- [ ] The registered player count updates immediately on the page after toggling.
- [ ] Registration is open for upcoming matches only; the register action is disabled for past matches.
