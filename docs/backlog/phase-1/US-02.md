# US-02: Select Who Paid

**Epic:** Session Initialization

**As a** team captain,
**I want to** select the group member who paid the court fee,
**so that** Splitwise records the correct payer and credits them accordingly.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A "Paid by" dropdown or selector is displayed alongside the Total Cost field.
- [ ] The dropdown is populated from the Splitwise group member list.
- [ ] Exactly one member can be selected as the payer.
- [ ] The field is required — proceeding without a selection is blocked.
- [ ] The selected payer is pre-checked in the attendance list automatically.
