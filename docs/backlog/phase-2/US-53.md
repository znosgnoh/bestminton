# US-53: Resolve Bets via Pari-mutuel Payout

**Epic:** Match Resolution

**As a** system,
**I want** to redistribute tokens from the losing pool to the winning pool,
**so that** bettors are paid proportionally to their stake.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] On `COMPLETED`, sum tokens in Pool A and Pool B.
- [ ] Bettors on the **losing** side lose their staked amount (deducted from `tokenBalance`).
- [ ] Total tokens from the losing pool are distributed **proportionally** among bettors on the winning side (by share of winning pool).
- [ ] Winners' `tokenBalance` increases by their payout; losers' decreases by their bet amount.
- [ ] Transaction is atomic (all balance updates succeed or none).
- [ ] Completed challenge UI lists each bettor: bet side, stake, payout or loss.
